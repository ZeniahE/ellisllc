About The Founder

Leighanne Ellis is a Realtor, investor, and financial strategist with a mission: to help individuals and families build real, lasting wealth through smart real estate decisions. With properties spanning Florida and Georgia, she brings hands-on experience as a landlord, property manager, and advisor to every client relationship.

From working with U.S. federal agencies to advising first-time buyers, Leighanne blends corporate precision with a personal touch—offering tailored guidance in buying, selling, investing, and wealth planning. A Marquis Who's Who in America inductee and proud member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., she is also a passionate advocate for financial literacy and generational wealth in underserved communities.

Driven by faith, ongoing education, and a commitment to excellence, Leighanne is known for turning intimidating financial goals into achievable milestones. Whether you're ready to buy your first property or expand your investment portfolio, she'll help you make confident, strategic decisions that build long-term value.
